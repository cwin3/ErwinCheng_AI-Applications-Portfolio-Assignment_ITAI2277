# Model Evaluation Notes

## Why Evaluation Matters
You need to measure how good your model actually is.

## Metrics I Used

### MAE (Mean Absolute Error)
- Average error between prediction and actual value
- Easy to understand

### RMSE (Root Mean Squared Error)
- Penalizes bigger mistakes more
- Good for spotting large errors

## My Results
- MAE ≈ 2.24
- RMSE ≈ 3.32

## What I Learned
- Lower error = better predictions
- No model is perfect, especially with real-world data
